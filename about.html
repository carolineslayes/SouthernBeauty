Protecting Our Environment

92 million tons of garment and textile waste is discarded annually. In an effort to reduce these numbers, we have found alternative ways to consume clothing. One way is renting. Rather than buying a dress and using it a few times then discarding, renting offers an opportunity to wear your dress and pass it on. Our rental dresses are fresh and new every time, giving you the same beauty as a dress brand new.
