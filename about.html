Who are we?

Southern Grace is a dress company that offers quality dresses at a low price.The dresses we offer come in a variety of sizes.Our store is family owned and we work with our local community.

What is our purpose?

Southern Grace seeks to provide dresses for those who want to spend less money on amazing dresses. By renting, you can use more dresses for more events, and expand your stylistic horizons!
